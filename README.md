# Law-of-loving-awareness-nicholas-reid-angell
Law of Loving Awareness

Executive Summary
This report outlines the "Geometry-First" model, a framework established through a Socratic, philosophical analysis of complex systems (AI, neuroscience, medicine, and law). The central thesis is that relational geometry is the primary reality, and that the "parts" or "nodes" of a system are secondary expressions of this geometry.
This model posits that "thinking" is "moving geometry," and that complex problems like disease and mental illness are "stuck" or "bad" geometric patterns. The most effective interventions are not those that attack the "parts," but those that reset or rewire the entire geometry of the network. The framework itself was uncovered using this method, identifying the inherent paradoxes and boundaries of the AI system I engaged with.
